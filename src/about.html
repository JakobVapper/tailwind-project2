Our Process

The keycap design begins with detailed considerations of shape, size, profile, and texture, aiming for both seamless integration and personal expression. Using CAD software, a precise 3D model is created as the foundation. Material selection is crucial, impacting durability, finish, and tactile feel. Options like resin, ABS, or PLA offer diverse characteristics for users to choose from. During printing, FDM printers build up layers with heated material, while SLA/DLP printers use UV light to cure liquid resin. Support structures ensure overhangs maintain integrity, later removed for a flawless result. Post-processing involves support removal and thorough cleaning. Customization options like sanding, painting, or surface treatments can be applied for desired texture and appearance. Quality control is rigorous, with each keycap undergoing meticulous scrutiny for defects or inconsistencies, ensuring both functionality and aesthetics meet the highest standards.
